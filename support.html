Common Questions

How do I back up my data?

Go to Me tab → Back up data. Save the file to iCloud Drive or Files. To restore, tap Restore from backup and select the file.

I switched phones. How do I get my data back?

If you backed up before switching, install the app on your new phone, go to Me → Restore from backup, and select your backup file from iCloud Drive.

How do I restore my Pro purchase?

Go to any locked feature (Routes, Nutrition) → tap "See options" → tap "Restore purchases" at the bottom. Your purchase is linked to your Apple ID.

Where is my data stored?

All data stays on your device. Nothing is sent to any server. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I cancel my Pro subscription?

Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Martin's Training Diary → Cancel. You keep Pro access until the end of your current billing period.
