Privacy Policy

Martin's Training Diary  ·  Last updated: March 27, 2026

Short version: Martin's Training Diary stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no servers, no analytics.

1. Information We Collect

Martin's Training Diary does not collect any personal information. All data you enter — workouts, body measurements, nutrition logs, weight history — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's on-device storage (AsyncStorage). This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly use the Export feature.

2. Data You Enter

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Workout logs (exercises, sets, reps, weights, duration, GPS routes)
  • Body measurements (weight, height, body fat %, muscle mass)
  • Nutrition logs (food entries, macros, calorie goals)
  • Profile information (age, gender, activity level, fitness goals)
  • InBody scan data

None of this data is transmitted to any server or third party.

3. Apple Health (HealthKit)

Martin's Training Diary can read data from Apple Health with your explicit permission. This includes:

  • Workouts recorded by Apple Watch
  • Resting heart rate and workout heart rate
  • Step count
  • Active energy burned
  • Distance (walking, running, cycling, swimming)

Health data is read from HealthKit and stored locally on your device only. It is never uploaded, shared, or transmitted to any external service. You can revoke Health permissions at any time in your iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Health.

4. Location Data

Martin's Training Diary can access your location during a workout to track GPS routes (distance, pace, speed). Location data is stored locally on your device as part of your workout history. Location access is optional — the app functions fully without it. You can revoke location permissions at any time in your iPhone Settings.

5. Camera

The camera is used exclusively to scan barcodes when adding food items. No photos or video are recorded or stored. Camera access is optional.

6. Third-Party Services

Martin's Training Diary does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs. There are no third-party services that receive your data.

7. Data Export

The app includes an Export feature that allows you to export your data as a JSON file. This export is initiated solely by you and goes directly to a destination you choose (e.g. Files app, email). We do not receive a copy.

8. Children's Privacy

Martin's Training Diary does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app does not collect any data from any user regardless of age.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
